Research Scholar

Welcome. I am a research scholar in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kanpur. Prior to joining IIT, I was a senior associate analyst at Infosys Limited. I am currently working with Prof. Dootika Vats. My primary area of research is in Markov chain Monte Carlo and output analysis. More details are in my CV.

Previously, I was a visiting student researcher with the Fulbright Nehru doctoral research fellowship at the School of Statistics, University of Minnesota Twin Cities, under the supervision of Prof. Galin L. Jones. Currently, I have submitted my PhD thesis and am actively looking for Postdoctoral positions.
